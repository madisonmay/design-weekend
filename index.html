Event Summary

Rethink Education is a weekend workshop crafted to give local undergraduates an opportunity to think critically about revamping the K-12 education space through user-oriented design. Under the mentorship of top New England designers, educators, and innovators, you will collaborate with equally passionate students to bring your ideas for change closer to reality. Register now to attend this exciting event hosted at Olin College!

FAQ

What's the cost?

The event is free for undergraduate students, and $10 for everyone else. Trust us, it'll be worth it.

Why design for education?

Olin College was founded 10 years ago with the goal of revolutionizing engineering education, and we'd like to spread that spirit of educational experimentation.

How can I get to Olin?

If you can't drive to 1000 Olin Way, Needham, MA, we will be running a shuttle to and from the Eliot T Station (Green D Line).

Do I need a team?

Nope! We'll be sending out a preregistration survey to help you set up teams if you don't have one.

Who can participate?

The event is for current undergraduate students, but we're also looking for professional designers, K-12 students, and teachers to help make the event possible.

How large will the event be?

We're lmiting this event to the first 100 undergraduate students, so sign up quick!
